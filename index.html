This is the first wallpaper I ever made combined with a editted RL logo.
Every letter in the wallpaper is entirely made by me using squares and triangles which cost me a lot of time. For that reason I always reuse these letters when spelling my name for future projects. I also use them as a signature when it's not used (examples follow). For the background I did the following: first I rendered clouds with white and light grey and then I crystalized it (a distort option in paint.net) which gave me this effect.
